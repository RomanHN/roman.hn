  • popular interests of CHS seniors – My school sent out a survey to seniors, and one of the questions asked responders to list some of their interests.   • printable EFF second short word list – The EFF (Electronic Frontier Foundation) created three lists of words to be used to generate passphrases by rolling dice. This is a printable version of their second short word list, the one with the unique three-character prefixes. The words are separated into six pages with six columns each, easing the process of passphrase generation.
